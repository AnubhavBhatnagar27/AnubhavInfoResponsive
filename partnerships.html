About SuperProcure

SuperProcure is a leading logistics and supply chain management solutions provider to manufacturing and infra companies. Our industry recognized products such as Spot Freight Negotiation, Trip Management System, Contract Management, Dispatch Planning and Freight Accounting are solving key industry problems and improving the quality of lives of people associated with logistics and supply chain management. We have a Pan-India customer base with a good mix of enterprise and SME/MSME customers who use our platform on a daily basis and boost their customer serviceability with cutting edge technology and solutions.
